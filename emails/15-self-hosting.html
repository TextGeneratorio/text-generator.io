Self-Host Text Generator on Your Own Servers

Want full control over your AI infrastructure? Run Text Generator on your own servers — your data never leaves your network, you control latency, and you own the costs.

Deployment Options

Docker

Pull the official image and run with a single command. Configure via environment variables. Works on any machine with Docker and a GPU.

Kubernetes

Helm charts for production-grade deployment. Auto-scaling, health checks, rolling updates. Run multiple instances behind a load balancer for high availability.

Why Self-Host?

Data

Full control. Your prompts and outputs never leave your infrastructure.

Latency

Co-locate with your app. Eliminate network round-trips to external APIs.

Cost

Per-instance pricing. Predictable costs that scale with your hardware, not your tokens.
